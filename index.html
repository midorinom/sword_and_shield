Sword and Shield

Your Action Gauge.

It fills up over time.

When one bar is full,

you can attack/defend.
Enemy Action Gauge.

When one bar is full,

the enemy attacks you.

The icons show the

enemy's next attacks.
Your Attack Button.

Press it to attack. Breaks

you out of defend stance

if you were defending.
Your Defend Button.

The next enemy attack

that hits you will have

its damage reduced by

70%.
When defending an attack, you get to play a minigame. You need to either (1) press 3 keys OR (2) click 3 boxes within 1.5 secs.

If successful, you will parry the attack.
